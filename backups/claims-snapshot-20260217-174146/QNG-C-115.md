## Claim Statement

Artificial stability gradients may lens light or particles for sensing and communication.

## Assumptions

- Artificially imposed stability gradients can be maintained over a measurable path length.
- Light/particle probes couple to `Sigma_art` through the same first-order lensing closure used in QNG gravitational optics.
- Background refractive and instrumental effects can be separated from QNG-style gradient effects.
