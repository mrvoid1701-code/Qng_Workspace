## Claim Statement

The Pioneer anomaly and spacecraft flyby anomalies arise as first-order consequences of stratonic lag dynamics, reflecting delayed gravitational response to moving mass distributions within the stability-field framework.

## Assumptions

- A1. Gravitational response is mediated by the stability field Σ\SigmaΣ rather than instantaneous mass position alone.
- A2. Stability-field evolution exhibits finite relaxation delay τ\tauτ dependent on informational load.
- A3. Moving spacecraft experience residual forces due to lag between instantaneous gravitational configuration and stability-field adaptation.
- A4. Stratonic lag produces measurable acceleration deviations in dynamic gravitational environments.
- A5. Observed anomalies are not fully explained by conventional forces alone.
