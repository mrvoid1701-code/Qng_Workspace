## Claim Statement

Spacetime is fundamentally composed of discrete informational nodes connected through relational links, with the apparent continuum description arising only as a macroscopic effective limit of large node ensembles.

## Assumptions

- A1. There exists a finite information density per physical region, implying a minimal representational scale.
- A2. Physical states can be represented as configurations of relational units (nodes) and their connections.
- A3. Continuum spacetime descriptions are emergent approximations valid at scales much larger than the node scale.
- A4. Node interactions are local within the relational graph topology.
- A5. Observable physical entities correspond to stable, persistent node configurations under update dynamics.
