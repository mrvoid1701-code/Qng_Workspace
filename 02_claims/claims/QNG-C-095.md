## Claim Statement

## Assumptions

- A1. Node phase ϕ\phiϕ evolves through stochastic update processes.
- A2. Ensemble behavior of many nodes produces probabilistic dynamics.
- A3. Phase coherence among nodes generates interference phenomena.
- A4. Coarse-graining over microscopic updates yields continuous evolution equations.
- A5. Observable quantum behavior reflects emergent ensemble statistics rather than fundamental wavefunctions.
