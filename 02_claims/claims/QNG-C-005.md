## Claim Statement

Matter, mass, and spacetime curvature are not fundamental entities but emerge from the collective evolution and stability properties of node states within the relational network.

## Assumptions

- A1. Nodes evolve according to local update rules influenced by neighboring states and stochastic fluctuations.
- A2. Stable node configurations form persistent structures identifiable as physical matter.
- A3. Mass corresponds to the temporal stability or persistence of node configurations.
- A4. Effective spacetime curvature arises from spatial gradients in stability and connectivity within the network.
- A5. Macroscopic physical laws emerge through coarse-graining of large ensembles of node interactions.
