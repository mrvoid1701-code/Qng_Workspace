## Claim Statement

The QNG framework is falsifiable through experimentally testable deviations from predictions of General Relativity (GR) and particle dark matter models, arising from stability-field memory dynamics and lag effects.

## Assumptions

- A1. QNG produces quantitative predictions distinguishable from GR and ΛCDM baselines.
- A2. Memory and lag dynamics introduce measurable deviations under specific conditions.
- A3. Classical gravitational behavior is recovered in appropriate limits.
- A4. Observational or experimental measurements can resolve predicted differences.
- A5. Competing models provide well-defined baseline predictions for comparison.
