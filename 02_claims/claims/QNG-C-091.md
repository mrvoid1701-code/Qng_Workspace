## Claim Statement

If gravitational-wave propagation includes relaxation delay effects governed by τ(χ)\tau(\chi)τ(χ), then ringdown signals from compact-object mergers should exhibit distortions whose magnitude scales with system mass asymmetry and the associated informational delay.

## Assumptions

- A1. Gravitational dynamics are mediated by the stability field with finite relaxation delay.
- A2. Informational load χ\chiχ influences propagation response through τ(χ)\tau(\chi)τ(χ).
- A3. Asymmetric mass configurations generate stronger stability-field gradients and delayed responses.
- A4. Ringdown signals are sensitive to small deviations from classical propagation.
- A5. Observed gravitational waves reflect coarse-grained stability dynamics.
