## Potential Falsifier

- Experimental evidence showing strictly instantaneous gravitational response with no measurable delay.
- Observations incompatible with any time-shift approximation of the gravitational field.
- Demonstration that delay-based corrections cannot reproduce observed dynamics.
- Empirical necessity for purely instantaneous field evolution.

## Evidence / Notes

- Provides a practical approximation for modeling lag effects in dynamic systems.
- Conceptually consistent with relaxation processes in physical systems.
- Supports interpretation of residual accelerations as delay-induced effects.
- Empirical validation depends on detecting measurable deviations from instantaneous response.
