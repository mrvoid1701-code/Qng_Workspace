## Claim Statement

- A2. A rapid synchronization transition occurred when stability conditions crossed a critical threshold.
- A3. Coherence propagation can occur faster than effective causal transport due to relational update processes.
- A4. Large-scale homogeneity results from synchronization rather than geometric inflation.
- A5. Observable cosmological features emerge from coarse-grained network dynamics.
