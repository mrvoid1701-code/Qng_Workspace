## Claim Statement

The QNG framework suggests that resonant node configurations and controlled update dynamics could enable programmable physical behavior and topology-based computation, where information processing is implemented through engineered stability patterns and network connectivity.

## Assumptions

- A1. Node states and update rules can exhibit resonant or periodic behavior.
- A2. Stability patterns can encode persistent informational structures.
- A3. Network topology influences dynamical evolution and information flow.
- A4. Controlled configurations could implement computational operations.
- A5. Macroscopic observables correspond to underlying node-network behavior.
